Site Plan for Epic Reviews

Site Name

Epic Reviews: This name represents a space where users can read and share reviews about products, books, and movies. It is clear, attractive, and relevant to the purpose of the site.

Suggested domain: epicreviews.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to provide a platform where users can find and share reviews of products, books, and movies, helping others make informed decisions.

Scenarios

  • What are the most popular reviews?
  • How can I add my own review?

Color Schema

Selected colors:

  • Blue (#1E90FF): For headers and links.
  • Light gray (#F5F5F5): For backgrounds and sections.

Typography

Selected fonts:

  • Poppins: For headers.
  • Roboto: For body text.
